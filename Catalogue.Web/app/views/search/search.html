Find, access and manage the data assets we hold as an organisation


This is the JNCC catalogue – and it's being built:

  • to give us a better foundation for our evidence base
  • to help us work more effectively, by
    • knowing which datasets we've got
    • helping you find the datasets you need
    • helping you share knowledge – between current staff and when you leave
    • encouraging teams to better organise their own data resources
    • improving our ability to serve external requests
    • avoiding asking other organisations for the same data more than once
    • solving practical problems around dataset licensing and terms of use
  • to allow us to benefit from a shared metadata standard, by
    • making contract deliverables easier to specify
    • sharing metadata with other organisations
    • contributing to other metadata catalogues and portals
  • to satisfy external quality requirements
  • to help teams towards their open data responsibilities

We think that there's a clear, common need in many teams that we can meet with a shared solution. The catalogue is in the early phases of development. Please check out our roadmap (right) and get in touch at pete.montgomery@jncc.gov.uk if you want to help influence the project.

Full text search New

Check out our super-fast, super-easy, full-text search. It's been designed to make finding datasets in the catalogue about as easy as if you were using Google.

You can currently search all the datasets from the Mapping European Seabed Habitats (MESH) project. Many thanks to Helen Ellwood for helping us import this really useful seed data.
